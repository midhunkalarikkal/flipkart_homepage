No Cost EMI

No Cost EMI, or Equated Monthly Installment, is a financial offering that has revolutionized the way consumers purchase products and services. With No Cost EMI, customers can spread the cost of their purchases over several months without incurring any additional interest charges. Essentially, the consumer pays the exact product price divided by the chosen tenure, making high-value purchases more affordable and manageable. This financial flexibility enables individuals to acquire goods they might otherwise find financially burdensome upfront. It's a win-win solution for both consumers and sellers, enhancing affordability and accessibility while facilitating smoother transactions in the world of retail.

Mobile Exchange Offer

A mobile exchange offer is a promotional initiative provided by retailers or e-commerce platforms that allows customers to exchange their old or used mobile phones for a new one, usually at a discounted price. The value of the discount or offer is determined based on the make, model, age, condition, and specifications of the old mobile device being traded in. This exchange offer presents several advantages to customers. It allows them to upgrade to a newer and more advanced mobile phone while reducing the overall cost of the purchase. It also encourages responsible disposal of old electronic devices by providing an incentive for recycling.Moreover, mobile exchange offers contribute to a more sustainable environment by promoting the proper disposal and recycling of electronic waste, aligning with a broader global effort towards eco-friendliness and waste reduction.

Frequntly Asked Questions

To track your order on Flipkart, log in to your account, go to 'My Orders,' and click on the order you want to track. You'll find the tracking information and current status of your shipment.

To return a product, go to 'My Orders,' select the item you want to return, and follow the instructions to initiate the return process. Make sure to review the return policy for eligibility and guidelines.

Flipkart Plus is a loyalty program offering benefits like faster delivery, early access to sales, and more. You can join by earning Plus Coins through purchases, and once you accumulate enough coins, you'll be automatically enrolled in the program.

While making a purchase, choose the 'No Cost EMI' option during payment. Select the EMI tenure and bank, and the EMI amount will be added to your monthly bill, with no additional interest.

You can change your delivery address before the item is shipped. Go to 'My Orders,' select the order, and click on 'Change Address' to update your delivery details.

Flipkart accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, Flipkart Gift Cards, Cash on Delivery (COD), and more.
